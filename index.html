    A little bit about me,

    I am a recent graduate from the University of Leeds, where I studied a Bachelors in Physics as well as a Masters in Data Science and Analytics. Achieving a first and a distinction respectively. My academic journey was not only about mastering complex subjects but also about embracing new experiences and opportunities for growth.

    One of these being sport, I have been into sport since I can remember, playing rugby, football and many other sports. However, throughout my time at Leeds I decided to try new sports such as, boxing, squash, and American football as I am an inquisitive person by nature and I love to learn new things.

    However due to an unfortunate accident I am no longer able to play contact sports. This led me to discover a newfound passion for fitness and the gym, fostering not only physical well-being but also social connections. Pursuing courses in personal training and nutrition further deepened my understanding of health and wellness.

    As well as sports and fitness I am also fascinated by travel and different cultures around the world. My most recent travel involved my friends and I travelling around North America in an RV, covering 40 states. This trip was life changing, not only from the memories made but also the skills and lessons developed. A new sense of responsibility and independence as well as an increased ability to perform well under pressure, which has facilitated the development of my interpersonal and financial skills that is now use daily. On top of this, experiencing new cultures and perspectives has expanded horizons has reshaped me into a better and more well rounded citizen.

    This journey of education and further interests has led me to want to pursue a career in the technology sector. With a strong foundation in programming, mathematics, and data analysis, I'm ready to apply my skills to real-world problems and contribute to meaningful projects. Having just come out of university, I am hoping for an entry level position where I can broadcast my fresh skills and new ideas.

    As you browse through my portfolio, I hope you'll gain insight into my background, skills, and aspirations. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect or learn more about my work. Thank you for visiting!
